F.A.Q

  • What is Youthfest?

    A 15 day long funfest organized by Oasis highlighting the "The Journey from Depression to Hope" through various events.

  • Why Youthfest?

    A memorable fest FOR & BY the students for helping grads out for making a move about their life and to come out of the darkness.

  • What are the Advantages of Youthfest?

    An opportunity for the generation to socially interact with new people, to share ideas, to make new bonds, and to build new relations.
    A platform to share new ideas, intriguing stories, excellent talents.

  • Happenings in Youthfest ?

    A new gen can now take part in the E Sports sector, an upcoming hype sector for the new world to take the competitive spirit to newer heights.
    ROAD SHOWS, FLASH MOBS, heating up the stage with your twisty DANCE moves, revving up the engines with BIKE SHOWS, many RALLIES and what so...

  • Is there any Ticket Reservation in Youthfest ?

    YES! For most events, unless for specific workshops, most of the eventss are completely free of cost.
    Only Students from the University with your ID pass will let you get access to all the events.

  • Can I cancel my Tickets after registering for a particular event ?

    All tickets are non-refundable and non-transferable. Tickets cannot be applied to future events.

  • Availability of Medical and Health Care?

    Volunteers will guide you to the rest houses and proper medical kits with doctor guidance.
